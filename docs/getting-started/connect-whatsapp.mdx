# Connect WhatsApp

On the left panel, you should see “Whatsapp” menu, click on it. If your whatsapp is not linked yet, link it.

### Step 1: View QR Code
![Click Scan Code](/img/tutorial/connect-whatsapp/connect-whatsapp-click-scan-code.png)

On the left panel, you should see “Whatsapp” menu, click on it. Once the WhatsApp connection page is shown, click on "Scan Code".



### Step 2: Scan QR Code
![QR Code](/img/tutorial/connect-whatsapp/connect-whatsapp-scan-your-whatsapp-code.png)
After clicking on "Scan Code" you should see the QR code. Use your WhatsApp app on your mobile phone to scan the QR code shown.

### Step 3: WhatsApp Connected
![WhatsApp Connected](/img/tutorial/connect-whatsapp/connect-whatsapp-verify-whatsapp-is-connected.png)
Once you have linked your WhatsApp to ExaBloom, you should see your number showing on the screen.
